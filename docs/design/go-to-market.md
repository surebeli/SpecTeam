# PhoenixTeam Go-to-Market Strategy

## Business Model

| Tier | Content | Price |
|------|---------|-------|
| Freemium | Open-source core (MCP Server + basic sync scripts) | Free for ≤5 members self-hosted |
| Team Pro | Dashboard, advanced arbitration, auditing | $15/user/month |
| Enterprise | Custom (Private deployment + deep Yunxin integration + support) | Annual fee + professional services |

## Promotion Phases

### Awareness (Months 0-3)

- Open-source MCP core on GitHub
- Zhihu/CSDN/Dev.to series of articles "The End of the Multi-AI Tool War"
- Demo videos (5 Agents converging in 30 minutes)

### Acquisition (Months 3-6)

- Invite-only Beta (for heavy Claude/Cursor users)
- NetEase Yunxin ecosystem promotion (client lists, joint webinars)
- X / Xiaohongshu / WeChat Video Account case studies

### Conversion & Retention

- Landing page with free trial
- Community building (Discord/WeChat groups)
- Partnerships (Cursor ecosystem, IDE vendors, NetEase sales team)

## Revenue Projection

- First year: 100 teams x $20k ARR = $2M
- 10x growth within 3 years

## KPIs

- 500 Stars, 50 Beta teams within 3 months
- NPS > 80
